Intro

Hi, This is Arnab Banerjee, I am a Software Architect by profession and I love to learn new technologies. I am father of a loving daughter and when I am not working, I love to spend time with my family. By the way, check out my awesome work.

I am also a movie buff and love documentaries. Documentaries that address social issues are my favorite, but I also love watching any content based movie. Traveling is my hobby, so whenever I get chance I visit new places with my family. Mountains, Seas, National Parks all are my favorite, I crave for sereneity of nature. I love to socialize with people, so when time permits I explore new restaurants and different cuisines with my family and friends.

Work

I am a Solution Architect at Visa Inc; I have over a decade experience in Electronic Payment Systems. I am a Subject Matter Expert in VISA's Network Processing Applications. I am currently pursuing MBA from McCombs School of Business at UT Austin (Class of 2021). I have successfully completed PMC Level II Certification from Pragmatic Institute. I am certified Scrum Master and Member of Scrum Alliance Community. I have software development expertise in building and maintaining application in C/C++ on IBM Z/OS Operating Systems. Currently I am dipping my toe into microservices architecture and cloud computing.

If you like my work and want to connect with me please visit the Contact page.
